Our Process

From Research to Reality

1

Colony Identification

We use satellite imagery, ecological surveys, and ground-penetrating radar to identify optimal colony locations with extensive tunnel networks.

2

Non-Invasive Mapping

Our sensors create detailed 3D maps of tunnel systems without disturbing the colony, identifying key chambers and traffic patterns.

3

Communication Protocol Analysis

We decode the specific pheromone signals used by this colony, building a communication library for two-way interaction.

4

Symbiotic Integration

Micro-sensors and interface nodes are carefully integrated into existing infrastructure, enhancing rather than replacing natural systems.

5

Network Activation

The colony becomes a node in our global network, sharing data and resources while maintaining full autonomy and health.

6

Continuous Monitoring

We monitor colony health, infrastructure integrity, and ecosystem impact to ensure our presence benefits the entire system.
